AUCTION RULES

Each team will have a purse of 80cr. prior to auction.Each team can bid a maximum of 20 players and a maximum of 6 overseas players.The minimum team stregnth must be 15.Each player will have a base price set to them starting from 50 lakhs to 2cr. for MARQUEE PLAYERS.

ABOUT THE TOURNAMENT

The Indian Cricket Premier League (ICPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested during March or April and May of every year by six teams representing six different cities in India.The league was founded by MOHITH G.G and DADU ALLU in 2019. ICPL has an exclusive window in ICC Future Tours Programme. One team can have a maximum of 4 overseas player in their playing XI.
