SUNRISERS HYDERABAD(SRH)

The SunRisers Hyderabad (often abbreviated as SRH) are a franchise cricket team based in Hyderabad, India, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL).[3] The franchise is owned by Kalanithi Maran of the Sun TV Network and was founded in 2012 after the Hyderabad-based Deccan Chargers were terminated by the IPL.[4] The team is currently captained by Kane Williamson and coached by Trevor Bayliss.[5] The primary homeground of the team is the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium, Hyderabad with a capacity of 55,000.[6] The brand value of the SunRisers Hyderabad was estimated to be ₹483 crore (US$70 million) in 2019 as the overall brand of IPL was increased to ₹47,000 crore (US$6.8 billion), according to Duff & Phelps.[7]
